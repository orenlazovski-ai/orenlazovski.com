Biography

Oren Lazovski is a choreographer, ballet master, rehearsal director, and former professional dancer with extensive experience across leading European and Israeli dance institutions. His work blends musicality, theatrical sensitivity, and a deep understanding of movement language.

A graduate of the Palucca Hochschule für Tanz Dresden (MA Dance Pedagogy), Oren has performed and collaborated with companies and artists across Germany, the Netherlands, Israel, and beyond.

Selected Works

  • 2024 – "Happy End", Kamea Dance Company
  • 2019 – "Zeit", Palucca University Dresden
  • Choreographic collaborations with Deutsche Oper Berlin, Staatsoper Berlin, and international ensembles

Teaching & Masterclasses

Oren teaches Ballet, Modern, Choreographic Composition, and the innovative method "Strange Tools" focusing on choreomusical research. He has worked as guest teacher at major theaters including Staatstheater Hannover, Deutsche Oper Berlin, and many others.
