Oren Lazovski is an Israeli–German choreographer, ballet master, and rehearsal director, currently working with the Kamea Dance Company. A graduate of the Palucca Hochschule für Tanz Dresden (MA Dance Pedagogy), his career spans major European and Israeli dance companies, opera houses, and music ensembles.
His choreographic work is driven by a deep engagement with musical structure, physical clarity, and performative honesty. Alongside his stage work, Oren teaches ballet, contemporary technique, choreographic composition, and his research-based method Strange Tools, developed for dancers and musicians.
